Why You Should Care

Stress = Lower Self Esteem

Too much stress results in exhaustion. This exshaution will make you not think straight, making you prone to make more mistakes. As a result, you will begin to be too hard on yourself.

Stress = Suicide

We see that groups of people between ages 25-64 have the highest suicide rates. Many sources conclude that this is due to loss of self-sufficiency and loneliness.

Stress = Obesity

Stress can lead us to find different habits to relieve said stress. Some habits poeple turn to are unhealthy. There is a connection between obesity and high stress levels.
